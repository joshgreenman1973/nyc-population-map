New York City, by neighborhood

This is every neighborhood (NTA) in the five boroughs, colored by whatever variable you pick from the list on the left — population, race, income, rent, crime, school enrollment, election results, and more.

  • Pick a variable in the sidebar. The map and the citywide stats update live.
  • Switch to "Two variables" to color by two metrics at once on a 3×3 grid. Hover a legend cell to highlight only the neighborhoods in that bin.
  • Search any NYC address at the top of the map to find the containing tract or neighborhood.
  • Use Tract / Neighborhood to zoom in to census tracts when the bigger geography hides what you want.

All numbers come with caveats — see the methodology link in the footer for sources, margins of error, and the data behind the rent-stabilized counts.
